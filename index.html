Format

There will be two stages in the tournament:

  1. A group stage, where participants are split into groups of 5, where the top 2 players in each group will qualify for the next stage. Each group and it's players will be put in a table, where a win awards 3 points, and a loss awards 0 points. Participants in each group will play each other EXACTLY once, first to 10.
  2. A knockout stage It is single elimination
